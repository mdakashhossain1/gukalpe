Ek aur important improvement
Aapke current plan me "Daily Accrual" aur "Maturity Amount" dikhaya gaya hai. Agar aap future me real users ke saath financial product launch karte hain, to fixed or guaranteed high returns (jaise 250%, 708%, 3000%) ko UI ya marketing me promise karna legal aur platform policy perspective se problematic ho sakta hai. Isliye architecture configurable rakhna sahi hai, lekin production version me returns, disclosures aur applicable regulations ko dhyan me rakhna zaroori hoga.
Mere hisaab se is Plan Page ko 10/10 premium fintech experience banane ke liye ye improvements kaafi strong foundation denge. Uske baad hum developer ke liye final implementation document aur uske matching premium UI design bana sakte hain.
